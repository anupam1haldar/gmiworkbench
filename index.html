Built for hospital operations

GMI brings the day-to-day work of running a hospital into a single system of record. Administrators get real-time visibility into capacity and staffing, clinical teams get faster access to the information they need, and leadership gets the reporting required to keep operations running smoothly and compliantly.

Feature highlights

Bed & capacity management

Track bed availability, admissions, and discharges in real time to keep units running at safe, efficient capacity.

Staff scheduling

Build and adjust shift schedules across departments, with visibility into coverage gaps before they become problems.

Patient flow coordination

Coordinate transfers, procedures, and discharges across teams so patients move through care without unnecessary delays.

Reporting & compliance

Generate operational and regulatory reports from a single source of truth, reducing manual reconciliation work.

Role-based access

Give administrators, clinicians, and support staff the views and permissions appropriate to their role.

Facility-wide dashboards

See census, staffing, and throughput at a glance, across one unit or the entire facility.

Who it's for

Hospital administrators, unit managers, and care coordination teams looking to reduce operational overhead.

How it fits in
